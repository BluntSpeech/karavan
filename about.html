ABOUT US

Why do I look like my father if I hate him? What does a typical day in a "gay conversion camp" look like? How does a private school break your mind and destroy your health?

We live in the era of post-truth. Bombarded with pre-packaged headlines, curated images, and articles that are designed to convince us of a perspective they want us to believe.

Stories that once mattered, spaces that once belonged to real people — hollowed out, repackaged, and sold back to us as propaganda.

As the editors of Karavan, we are fucking tired.

We miss the internet as it used to be.
And we want to bring back what it was made for — you.

Every article you see here is a story told by a real person. No hidden sponsors. No agendas. No editorial guidelines.

Listen to others without demanding answers.Take in experiences without feeling the need to give anything in return.

And if you ever feel like sharing — send us your story, and let it echo through the desert winds of this Karavan.
