We live in the era of post-truth. Believing the words on the other side of the screen is no longer possible. We log in and are bombarded with pre-packaged headlines, curated images, and articles that are designed to convince us of a single perspective. Stories that once mattered, spaces that once belonged to real people—hollowed out, repurposed, and sold back to us as propaganda.

As the editors of Karavan, we are fucking tired. If you're reading this—know that we are exhausted.

We miss the internet as it used to be. And we want to bring back what it was made for—you.

With your ideas, fears, struggles—but most importantly, with your opinion.

Every article you see here is a story told by a real person. No hidden sponsors. No agendas. No editorial "guidelines." We protect anonymity. We protect our writers.

Let go. Listen to others without demanding answers. Take in their experiences without needing something in return. And if you ever feel like sharing—send us your story.
