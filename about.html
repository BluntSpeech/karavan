ABOUT US

Why do fake research papers get published, while independent archives are shut down? Why do I look like my father if I hate him? What is it like to be homeless in New York?

Karavan is an experimental performance newspaper.

Every week, we send new stories straight to your inbox. Past stories remain in the archive—ready to be reread, shared, and discussed.

We, the editors of Karavan, remember the internet before it became a battlefield of algorithms, engagement traps, and curated realities. A time when stories were told, not sold.

Now, in an era of post-truth, mass surveillance, and speech restrictions, we want to bring back what the internet was made for—

People.
