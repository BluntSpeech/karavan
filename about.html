ABOUT US

We live in the era of post-truth. Believing the words on the other side of the screen is no longer possible. We are bombarded with pre-packaged headlines, curated images, and articles that are designed to convince us of a perspective they want you to believe..

Stories that once mattered, spaces that once belonged to real people—hollowed out, repackaged, and sold back to us as propaganda.

As the editors of Karavan, we are fucking tired.

We miss the internet as it used to be.
And we want to bring back what it was made for—you.

Every article you see here is a story told by a real person.
No hidden sponsors. No agendas. No editorial "guidelines."
We protect anonymity. We protect our writers.

Listen to others without demanding answers.
Take in their experiences without feeling the need to give anything in return.
And if you ever feel like sharing—send us your story.
